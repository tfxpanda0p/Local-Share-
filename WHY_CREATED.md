# Why Local-Share Was Created

Sharing data from a laptop or desktop computer to a mobile device is a very common task, but existing solutions often have significant downsides:

- **Third-Party Apps & Messaging Platforms (e.g., WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, etc.):** Using these requires an active internet connection. It is time-consuming as your data goes over the internet, and file sizes are often restricted. Most importantly, uploading your private files through external servers can be a data privacy risk.
- **USB Cables:** Physically grabbing a cable, plugging it into both devices, and navigating through file storage is inefficient, tedious, and sometimes obstructed by driver issues or broken cables.

## The Solution: Local-Share

**Local-Share** was built to solve these exact problems. 

- **Not cloud storage, not an external app.** 
- **High-speed direct transfers:** It works directly on your local network (Wi-Fi), allowing you to transfer data at the maximum speed your router supports. 
- **No limits:** You don't have to worry about the file size limit or internet data usage.
- **Simple & Easy:** No cables, no internet bandwidth used, and complete data privacy. 

Just a reliable, high-speed way to get data from point A to point B instantly!
